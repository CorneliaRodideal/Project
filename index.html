All about Our Store

Our open location in the Buiucani sector of Chisinau, on Paris St. offers you a wide range of coffee drinks, lemonades and low-alcohol cocktails. At the same time, we have a varied menu of artisanal desserts prepared from natural ingredients with lots of love. Our motto is

“We give taste and joy to people”

Stepping inside, you are enveloped in the inviting aroma of freshly baked cakes, creating an atmosphere that feels like a comforting hug. The interior is adorned with rustic charm, with exposed brick walls, wooden furniture and a patchwork of cozy nooks adorned with plush cushions. Soft jazz notes play in the background, creating a melodic backdrop for patrons engaged in quiet conversation or engrossed in the pages of their beloved books.
We are here to help you!

Services



Wi-Fi

Our cafe has all the necessary facilities to have a productive working day, and that includes Wi-Fi! Just ask the receptionist for the password.

Gluten/Lactose Free

We cater to any dietary needs and have a lot of different options for all kind of preferences. Additionally, all our ingredients are grown natually, with no added pesticides/fertilizers.

Private Booths

We undertand the need for privacy! That's why we have a special room with personal booths, which are reserved on a first come - first serve system.

Blankets

We are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. You can either ask for an increase in temperature, or a personal blanket. We have one for everyone!

Bundles

Are you here for the whole day, but don't want to worry about food? Ask for a whole day bundle and order the food ahead of time, so our waiters will bring it to you at specific times.
